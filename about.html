About Us

Engineering the future of building systems since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010 in Mumbai, ICG Engineering Solutions began with a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between architectural ambition and engineering reality. Our founder, an experienced mechanical engineer, recognized the need for an MEP consultancy that prioritized both technical excellence and environmental responsibility.

From a small dedicated team of 5 engineers, we have steadily grown into a powerhouse of over 120 professionals spread across multiple offices in India. Our growth has been organic, fueled entirely by client referrals and a reputation for delivering uncompromising quality.

Today, we are proud to have delivered over 500 successful projects across diverse sectors including commercial, healthcare, residential, and industrial facilities. We continuously invest in the latest technologies, embracing a BIM-first approach to ensure clash-free designs and seamless project execution.
