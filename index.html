WHO WE ARE

Engineering Excellence Since 2010

ICG Engineering Solutions is a premier MEP consultancy firm committed to designing innovative, sustainable, and code-compliant building systems. Since our founding in 2010, we have partnered with architects, developers, and contractors to deliver high-performance environments that prioritize occupant comfort and energy efficiency.

Our multidisciplinary team brings deep expertise in HVAC system design, electrical power distribution, plumbing and drainage engineering, and fire protection. We leverage cutting-edge BIM coordination tools to ensure seamless integration across all trades — reducing conflicts, accelerating timelines, and minimizing construction costs.

Sustainability is at the core of every project. From energy modeling and LEED compliance to rainwater harvesting and high-efficiency mechanical systems, we engineer buildings that perform better for people and the planet.
