Our Services

Comprehensive MEP engineering solutions for every building type

At ICG Engineering, we deliver end-to-end Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing design services that integrate seamlessly with architectural vision and structural requirements. Our multidisciplinary team leverages cutting-edge BIM technology and decades of hands-on expertise to engineer buildings that are efficient, sustainable, and code-compliant.

Mechanical / HVAC Design

Most Popular

Our mechanical engineering team designs high-performance HVAC systems that deliver optimal thermal comfort while minimizing energy consumption. From central chilled water plants to advanced variable refrigerant flow (VRF) systems, we engineer solutions tailored to each building's unique requirements—be it a high-rise office, a hospital operating theater, or a cleanroom manufacturing facility.

We integrate cutting-edge energy recovery technologies, building automation systems, and smoke management solutions to ensure every project meets the highest standards of efficiency, safety, and indoor air quality. Our designs are fully coordinated with architectural and structural elements using 3D BIM workflows.

  • Heating & Cooling Load Calculations
  • Central Plant Design
  • Air Distribution Systems
  • Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF)
  • Clean Room HVAC
  • Energy Recovery Systems
  • Building Automation Integration
  • Smoke Management

Electrical Systems Design

Our electrical engineering division delivers robust power distribution, intelligent lighting, and integrated low-voltage systems designed for reliability, safety, and energy efficiency. We handle everything from high-voltage switchgear and transformer sizing to smart building automation, ensuring uninterrupted power for mission-critical facilities.

Whether it's a data center requiring redundant UPS architecture, a hospital needing seamless emergency power transfer, or a commercial tower integrating solar photovoltaic systems, our designs are code-compliant and future-ready.

  • High & Low Voltage Distribution
  • Lighting Design & Controls
  • Emergency & Standby Power
  • Renewable Energy Integration
  • Lightning Protection
  • Earthing & Grounding
  • UPS System Design
  • Smart Building Systems

Plumbing Design

Our plumbing engineers design complete water supply, drainage, and specialty piping systems that ensure reliable, hygienic, and efficient water management throughout the building lifecycle. From domestic water distribution to complex medical gas networks, our designs consider water conservation, pressure management, and regulatory compliance at every stage.

We specialize in sustainable water strategies including rainwater harvesting, greywater recycling, and advanced water treatment systems—helping clients reduce their water footprint while maintaining the highest quality standards for occupant health and safety.

  • Domestic Water Supply
  • Sanitary & Storm Drainage
  • Hot Water Systems
  • Medical Gas Systems
  • Gas Piping Design
  • Rainwater Harvesting
  • Water Treatment Systems
  • Greywater Recycling

Fire Protection Engineering

Our fire protection engineers design life-safety systems that protect people, property, and business continuity. From automatic sprinkler systems and fire alarm networks to specialized suppression systems for data centers and industrial hazards, we deliver designs that meet the most stringent local and international fire codes.

Every design begins with a comprehensive fire risk assessment and includes smoke control strategies, emergency lighting layouts, fire pump sizing, and complete code compliance reviews. We work closely with fire authorities to ensure seamless approval and commissioning of all fire life safety systems.

  • Automatic Sprinkler Systems
  • Fire Alarm & Detection
  • Smoke Control Systems
  • Fire Pump Design
  • Special Hazard Suppression
  • Emergency Lighting
  • Fire Risk Assessment
  • Code Compliance & Review

Our Design Process

A proven methodology that delivers consistent results on every project

1

Discovery & Analysis

We begin by understanding your vision, project goals, and site conditions. Our team conducts thorough needs assessments, site surveys, and feasibility studies to establish a solid foundation for the design.

2

Concept Design

We develop preliminary MEP layouts and system selections, coordinating closely with architects and structural engineers to optimize space, performance, and budget from the earliest stages.

3

Detailed Engineering

Our engineers produce comprehensive construction documents, detailed BIM models, and technical specifications—fully coordinated across disciplines to minimize clashes and change orders on site.

4

Construction Support

We provide on-site supervision, respond to contractor queries, manage design changes, and oversee testing, commissioning, and handover to ensure systems perform as designed.
